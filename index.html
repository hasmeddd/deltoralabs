About Us

Deltora Labs is a subsidiary of Deltora Foundation, an organization that invests in pre-launch blockchain projects with thousands of members. Deltora Labs launched in Q1 2024, quickly building a strong global presence, with strategic relationships in key blockchain markets, especially in Turkey, China, Korea and Vietnam.

These connections help us provide our clients with easy access to fast-growing regions, allowing them to expand first in targeted local markets or scale globally, depending on their goals. We always put the interests and growth of our partners first.
